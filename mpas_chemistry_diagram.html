Scheme On/Off Switches

These character/logical options enable entire aerosol or gas subsystems and activate the corresponding packages and tracers.

Fire & Biomass Burning Emissions

Controls diurnal cycling, emission factors, scaling, online calculation, and fire feedback. Primarily applies when config_smoke_scheme ≠ off.

Deposition & Gravitational Settling

Dry deposition, wet deposition, and gravitational settling options applied across all active chemical tracers via nChem-dimensioned diagnostics.

Dust Emission Tuning

Scaling and physical correction factors for FENGSHA dust. Requires config_dust_scheme ≠ off.

Pollen Emission Tuning

Scale factors per pollen species and sub-particle rupture count. Requires config_pollen_scheme ≠ off.

Plumerise

Algorithm and physical parameter choices for fire smoke injection height. Requires config_smoke_scheme ≠ off.

Radiation, PBL Mixing & Flux Feedbacks

Aerosol direct radiative effect, enhanced PBL mixing, and fire heat/moisture flux feedbacks to the atmosphere.

Dimension & Count Options

Integer options that control array sizes. num_chem must equal the count of active scalars at runtime.
